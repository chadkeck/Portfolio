HAMMHR
HAMMHR is the world's first online virus detector to leverage HTML5's drag and drop feature.
Use the HAMMHR Webapp
HAMMHR leads the way into the future of virus detection. Because of HTML5's new features, no software needs to be installed on the client, and works in any web browser that is compliant with the standards.
HAMMHR also leads the industry in user privacy. The information needed by Team Cymru's servers to determine if the file is malicious is calculated on the client's machine. What this means is that no file contents, not even the file's name, are sent across the network.
