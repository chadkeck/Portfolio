WinMHR
WinMHR is a free virus detector for Windows XP and greater. I was hired by Team Cymru because of my skills with C++, Qt, Windows, and network protocols to create this application.
It protects hundreds of users each day from around the globe, and was awarded #4 of the top five best free security software utilities for 2010 from PC Advisor.
Visit the WinMHR Website
I spent a lot of effort making the User Interface sleek and easy to use. Many design choices were made to make the common tasks easy, and the difficult tasks possible.
