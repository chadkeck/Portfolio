Resound
Resound shows Team Cymru's malevolence map on an interactive globe and aggregates Team Cymru's news and twitter feeds.
Resound heavily modifies many basic Qt widgets in order to achieve a great visual experience. Text fades when stories switch and article descriptions appear to come from outer space.
