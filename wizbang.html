WizBang
The goal of WizBang is to help non-programmers understand programming.
WizBang is a visual programming language that compiles to working and valid C, C++, Python, and Java code. It's amazing.
It started as a project for a class at Iowa State University with me and two other undergrads. After the semester was over, and we got an 'A', I open-sourced WizBang under the GPL and continued development by myself.
I am electrified by WizBang's potential for changing how we teach programming.
It had over 3,000 downloads the day I announced a public beta.
